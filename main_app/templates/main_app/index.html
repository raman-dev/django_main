CONWAY'S GAME OF LIFE

about

Simulation of a 2D universe where only cells exist, based on John Conway's rules of life.
Every cell interacts with its eight immediate neighbours, the next generation of cells is calculated by the following:

  • Any live cell with two or three live neighbours survives.
  • Any dead cell with three live neighbours becomes a live cell.
  • All other live cells die in the next generation, all other dead cells stay dead.
